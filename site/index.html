The Firm

Stonewell Capital Partners is an independent advisory firm established to provide sophisticated strategic and financial advice throughout Latin America.

The firm operates with a simple philosophy: complex situations require experienced judgment, direct involvement and disciplined execution.

Unlike larger institutions, Stonewell maintains a limited number of engagements at any given time. This approach allows senior professionals to remain directly involved throughout each assignment and provides clients with a highly personalized level of service.

The firm's experience spans multiple industries and jurisdictions throughout Latin America, including natural resources, infrastructure, power generation, industrial operations, consumer sectors, entertainment and government-regulated industries.

The firm is organized as a private advisory practice and generally serves clients through referrals and established professional relationships.
